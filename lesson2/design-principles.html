Visual Hierarchy

New World
newworld.com

I believe the New World website is a great example of Visual Hierarchy because it shows you what is most important on the page and it makes it known to the viewer. The website shows you the game and what ever major event is going on at the time.

Hick's Law

Steam Store
store.steampowered.com

I think Steams store is an example of Hick's Law. I think a lot of websites that have items you can purchase follow the same rule. The more things the user can look at and pick from the longer they are to stay on the page, and companies want you to do this to intice you to buy their products.

White Space and Clean Design

Twitch.Tv
twitch.tv

Lastly, we have Twitch.Tv which does an amazing job at controlling and utilizing white space and overall has a clean design. Everything looks smooth, and well spaced out which provides for some nice eye candy!
