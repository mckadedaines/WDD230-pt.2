About Us

Since our founding in 1990, Bountiful Foods has been dedicated to bringing fresh, locally-sourced produce to the SoCal community. We believe in sustainable farming practices and fostering relationships with local growers to provide our customers with the highest quality fruits and vegetables.

Our History

In the heart of sunny Southern California, the Johnson family started a modest farm stand at a local farmers' market in 1990. With their fertile land, they cultivated a small range of organic fruits and vegetables that quickly gained popularity in the community. Their success allowed for growth, and by 1995, they were incorporating innovative, sustainable farming techniques. This commitment to sustainable agriculture was put to the test during the drought of the early 2000s. But through the introduction of a water recycling system, the Johnsons ensured Bountiful Foods thrived, even in adversity. With their resilience and reputation for fresh, sustainably grown produce, Bountiful Foods expanded. They opened their first store in 2010, with more following throughout Southern California. Today, Bountiful Foods remains steadfast in its mission: providing fresh, locally-sourced produce and nourishing the SoCal community, while advocating for sustainable farming. The journey continues as Bountiful Foods strengthens its commitment to health and sustainability, serving the SoCal community one fruit and vegetable at a time.
